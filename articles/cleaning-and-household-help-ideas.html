Household help

Cleaning and household help ideas

Helpful tasks with clear boundaries

Cleaning and household help can be deeply useful when someone is overwhelmed. The key is to offer clear, limited tasks and respect privacy.

Start with low-pressure tasks

  • Take out trash and recycling.
  • Wash dishes or run the dishwasher.
  • Fold laundry or change bedding if invited.
  • Water plants, pick up mail, or walk a dog.

Ask before entering spaces

Do not assume every room is open. Ask what areas are okay to enter and what should be left alone.

Bring supplies if needed

Paper towels, trash bags, dish soap, laundry detergent, and pet supplies can be useful without feeling too personal.

Good boundaries

  • Ask first
  • Choose one task
  • Finish fully
  • Do not judge the home
