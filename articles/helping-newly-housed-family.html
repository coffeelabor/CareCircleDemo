Housing transition

Helping a newly housed family settle in

Practical support for a fresh start

Helping a newly housed family settle in often means covering ordinary basics that make a new place feel livable. Ask what is needed before buying large items.

Start with essentials

  • Cleaning supplies, trash bags, paper goods, laundry detergent, and dish soap.
  • Basic pantry items, snacks, and easy meals for moving week.
  • Towels, bedding, hangers, storage bins, lamps, or small kitchen tools.

Coordinate before donating

Large donations can become difficult to store or move. Confirm sizes, preferences, and delivery timing first.

Respect dignity

Offer support without making assumptions about why someone needed help. Practical, quiet support can go a long way.

Useful basics

  • Paper goods
  • Cleaning supplies
  • Pantry staples
  • Simple kitchen items
