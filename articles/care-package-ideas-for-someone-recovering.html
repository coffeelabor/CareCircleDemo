Care packages

Care package ideas for someone recovering

Comfort and usefulness in one box

Care package ideas for someone recovering should be simple, useful, and easy to receive. A thoughtful package does not need to be large or expensive.

Practical items

  • Soft socks, lip balm, tissues, hand lotion, or unscented wipes.
  • Tea, electrolyte drinks, shelf-stable snacks, or easy breakfast items.
  • Notecards, stamps, chargers, puzzle books, or streaming gift cards.

For households with kids

Consider coloring books, simple crafts, snacks, or a family meal gift card. Choose items that do not need major cleanup or adult supervision.

What to avoid

Avoid strong scents, complicated projects, medical products, or anything that assumes details about the person's condition.

Simple formula

  • One comfort item
  • One practical item
  • One snack or drink
  • One short note
