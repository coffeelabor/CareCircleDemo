Food help

Meal and grocery support ideas

Practical ideas for everyday support

Meal and grocery support can be one of the easiest ways to help a household get through a difficult week. The goal is to make food simpler, not to create more decisions.

Before you shop or cook

  • Ask about allergies, dietary needs, favorite basics, and freezer space.
  • Confirm drop-off timing and whether a visit is welcome.
  • Label meals with the date and simple reheating instructions.

Easy grocery basics

Consider breakfast items, fruit, snacks, drinks, paper goods, pantry staples, and household basics. A grocery gift card can be more helpful than guessing.

Make it repeatable

If friends are coordinating meals, use a simple schedule so the household does not receive too much food at once.

Helpful items

  • Ready-to-heat meals
  • Freezer-friendly portions
  • Breakfast basics
  • Grocery gift cards
