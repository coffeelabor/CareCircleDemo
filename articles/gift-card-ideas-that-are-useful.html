Gift cards

Gift card ideas that are actually useful

Flexible help can be the easiest help

Useful gift cards can help someone cover practical needs without having to explain every detail. The best choice depends on what is available nearby and what the person already uses.

Flexible categories

  • Groceries and household basics.
  • Gas, rides, or transportation.
  • Meal delivery or local restaurants.
  • Pharmacy, big-box stores, or general prepaid cards.

Make it easy to accept

Pair the gift card with a short message and no request for updates. If possible, ask whether a physical card or digital card is easier.

Check local fit

A card is only helpful if the store, app, or service works in the person's area and is easy for them to use.

Good uses

  • Groceries
  • Gas
  • Meals
  • Household basics
