About the idea

Support should feel easier to organize.

My Care Circle is a proof of concept for a future community care site: part support guide, part resource library, and eventually a clearer way for loved ones to coordinate practical help.

Mission

Help people feel supported, not alone.

The mission is to give families, friends, neighbors, and community groups a simple place to find practical ways to help someone during a difficult season.

This first version focuses on content. It does not collect personal information or invite users to share health details. Future registry or profile features should be reviewed carefully before they are built.

POC boundaries

What this version does and does not do.

Does

Share general support ideas, care package suggestions, article starters, and links to reputable external resources.

Does not

Collect health information, host personal stories, accept payments, manage registries, create accounts, or offer medical advice.

Future review

Profiles, registries, wish lists, affiliate programs, comments, and contact forms should be considered only after legal, privacy, and content-policy review.
